05bCultural Cognition Engine— narrative persistence and memetic decay
CULTURAL_COGNITION_ENGINE
Narrative Persistence Model
CC = Frame + Meaning + Resonance + SymbolicWeight CC_{t+1} = ρ·CC_t − κ·Age ρ = narrative_persistence_coefficient (how "sticky" is the frame?) κ = decay_rate (how quickly does it lose symbolic weight?) Narrative_Persistence = CC_t > threshold for sustained price impact
Cultural Cognition governs how long a narrative continues to move capital after its structural basis. A CC with high ρ (sticky frame) persists even after the structural signal weakens — creating the temporal gap between structural truth and market price that the pipeline exploits. Low CC × high structural signal = PROMETHEUS_CONDITION (early entry justified). High CC × weakening structural = OVERRIPE state (exit approaching).
SURVIVORSHIP_CORRECTION
True Mastery Estimation
TrueSkill ≈ Observed − (Luck + Environment + SelectionBias) Mastery = (Skill × Robustness × Replicability) / (1 + ContextDependence) FailureDensity = failures_around_strategy / total_attempts Robustness = P(success | perturbed_conditions) ContextDependence: how much does performance require specific regime?
Before promoting any strategy to standard protocol: compute Mastery score. A strategy with positive E[CLV] but high ContextDependence is a regime-coincidence, not genuine edge. Low FailureDensity + high Mastery = generalizable. SPRT_VALIDATION should test Mastery × Replicability, not just sequential E[CLV]. SurvivorshipBias term in master equation denominator is 1/Mastery-adjusted for context-dependence.
06Power / Food Chain— actor dominance scoring and hierarchy
POWER_FOOD_CHAIN
Actor Dominance Scoring
FD_i = a₁·Capital + a₂·Information + a₃·Execution + a₄·Narrative FD_adj_i = FD_i × Robustness_i APEX: FD_adj > 8.0 (Starlizard, Renaissance, sovereign fund tier) DOMINANT: FD_adj 5–8 (institutional fund, major government, central bank) OPPORTUNISTIC: FD_adj 2–5 (informed retail, smaller fund, regional actor) VULNERABLE: FD_adj < 2 (narrative-only, uninformed, reactive capital)
The food chain determines whose capital flow validates a thesis vs whose capital flow is the crowded sidecar. When an APEX_ACTOR moves: TACIT_COORDINATION weight × 2.0. When OPPORTUNISTIC moves alone: noise probability elevated → reduce signal confidence. POWER_FOOD_CHAIN integrates with INCENTIVE_COMPATIBILITY: an APEX actor with IC_i=0.3 (bluffing) is the most dangerous setup — maximum structural power + deceptive public signal.
Classify every key actor by FD_adj before thesis entry. APEX + IC_i=1.0 → highest TACIT_COORDINATION weight. DOMINANT + IC_i<0.4 → PRINCIPAL_AGENT_FLAG active.
ACTOR_INCENTIVE_INTEGRATION
Combined Actor Assessment
U_i(a) = B_i − C_i + φ_i·S_i + ψ_i·R_i [incentive decomposition] IC_i = 1 − |a_public − a*| / Z [compatibility score] P(θ|m) = P(m|θ)P(θ) / Σ P(m|θ')P(θ') [type belief update] ActorSignalWeight = FD_adj_i × IC_i × CostlinessOfSignal CheapTalkDiscount: if signal_cost ≈ 0 → ActorSignalWeight × 0.3
Combines food chain position with incentive compatibility. A powerful actor sending cheap talk (IC=0, costless signal) gets near-zero signal weight regardless of their FD_adj. A weaker actor making a costly, credible commitment (IC=1.0, high cost to signal) gets elevated weight. This is the Spence signaling model applied: costliness is the credibility mechanism.

08Agent Selection System— strategy modes with switching, ensemble, fallback
AGENT_SELECTION_ENGINE
Strategy Mode Arbitration
Score_a = RewardFitness + SignalAlignment + TimingFit + ExecutionStrength − Risk − Friction A* = argmax_a Score_a SwitchingCost = c_switch (prevents churning) Switch only when: Score_{new} − Score_{current} > c_switch ENSEMBLE: run multiple agents with S9B correlation-adjusted sizing FALLBACK: fixed preset when CIE < 0.4 AND KALI_YUGA AND H(b_t) > 0.8
Agent selection is a superset of FIRE_MODE. SNIPER/M16/UZI are single-agent modes. ENSEMBLE deploys multiple simultaneously with portfolio interaction. FALLBACK is a minimal preset (no new entries, existing positions monitored, Gk role dominant) that activates when the primary agents are demonstrably unreliable. The switching cost prevents the system from thrashing between modes on small signal variations.
At each session: compute Score_a for all modes. Switch only if delta > c_switch. FALLBACK activates automatically when 3 conditions simultaneously hold.
AGENT_MODE_DEFINITIONS
Mode Specifications
SNIPER: H(b_t) LOW, single dominant setup, LP_conditions ≥ 4/6 M16: multiple corroborating signals, NARRATIVE phase, medium certainty UZI: high frequency, low conviction, mean-reversion regime only ENSEMBLE: portfolio correlation check (S9B) mandatory before activation MONITOR: observation only — DRIFT mode, no execution permission FALLBACK: fixed rules, Gk only, no new entries, existing positions triage
MONITOR (DRIFT mode) and FALLBACK are new formal agents not previously named. MONITOR is the standard non-execution state during thesis development. FALLBACK is the automatic safe mode. Importantly: the pipeline should spend 60-70% of time in MONITOR or M16 (reconnaissance and preparation), not hunting for SNIPER entries. SNIPER is for the rare LEICESTER_PRIME condition — not for every week.

11Reality Validation (MILK TEST)— strategy promotion gate
MILK_TEST_REALITY_VALIDATION
Composite Reality Score
RealityScore = Edge × Discipline × Robustness × ExecutionQuality Edge: leave_score / 10 (theoretical edge) Discipline: PROCESS_QUALITY_SCORE / 5 (protocol adherence rate) Robustness: Mastery × Replicability (survivorship-corrected) ExecutionQuality: actual_CLV / expected_CLV (execution matched plan?) Comparison: expected_edge vs simulated_edge vs real_outcome
This is the gate every strategy must pass before promotion to standard protocol. It extends SPRT from a binary test to a composite score. RealityScore > 0.6 across T ≥ 10 in-sample + RealityScore > 0.5 across T ≥ 5 out-of-sample = PROMOTE. Score < 0.3 for 3 consecutive uses OR Robustness drop (regime changed) = DEMOTE. Every active strategy in the pipeline should have a live RealityScore visible in the Moltbook.
Before any protocol change promotion: compute MILK_TEST score. No gut-feel promotions. The SPRT tells you significance. The MILK_TEST tells you quality.
