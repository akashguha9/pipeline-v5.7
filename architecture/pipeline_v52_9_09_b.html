This is the first structured patch in the pipeline's history — not a philosophical spec but a technical upgrade with defined inputs, outputs, and processing rules. It adds a Step -1 layer that runs before any signal arrives. Previously the pipeline started at ingestion (signal received, begin processing). Now it starts at pre-signal tension assessment: is structural tension forming before capital moves and before language forms? The 5-dimension PRE_SIGNAL_TENSION_CHECK evaluates structural_mismatch, infrastructure_friction, trust_gradient, silent_positioning, and implementation_proximity before any market signal is considered. Never enter on pre-signal alone — it upgrades attention allocation and patience. It converts to a tradeable signal only when pre-signal builds to BUILDING/RELEASE_IMMINENT and then MID_TEMP signal + capital flow align on top.

OBSERVABLE_STATE_CLASSIFIER addresses the most dangerous failure mode in the pipeline: treating NARRATIVE_PROJECTED signals as OBSERVABLE_CONFIRMED. The 4-state taxonomy (Observable/Structural/Narrative/Mystical) forces explicit state declaration before evaluation proceeds. MYSTICAL_NOISE is discarded immediately. NARRATIVE_PROJECTED enters the shadow book as a watchlist item only. This is the MEASUREMENT_HUMILITY_RULE's companion gate — both stem from the same epistemic discipline: absence of visible signal ≠ absence of structure, but unexplained ≠ magical.

FLOW_DYNAMICS_ENGINE adds the VISCOSITY dimension that was missing. The pipeline had diffusion speed (narrative_contagion_rate) and movement type (war_style_classifier). What it lacked was a formal measure of what is SLOWING migration — legal barriers, infrastructure inertia, KYC friction, capital controls. High viscosity combined with strong structural mismatch = the IGNORED setup candidate. When pressure finally releases after high-viscosity accumulation, that is the dangerous_position trigger if all other gates align simultaneously. This is why LEICESTER_PRIME requires all 4 conditions — ignored + pre_signal building + drift strong + capital confirming — because the long viscosity period that produces IGNORED visibility is exactly what makes the eventual release asymmetric.
