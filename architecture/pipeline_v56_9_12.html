PERFORMANCE_LENS
S2+S1 · Attention quality
Measures whether crowd attention is coherent (focused, sticky, precise) or fragmented (erratic, fading, off-timing). Distinct from signal_density (how many signals exist) and pent_up_attention_score (how much suppressed attention). This measures the QUALITY of attention — is the crowd genuinely locked into this narrative or scattered? Focused+Sticky+Precise = crowd will sustain behaviour. Fragmented+Fading+Off = signal will not hold crowd long enough for capital flow.
Distinct from: pent_up_attention_score (quantity) · signal_density in CONTEXT_VECTOR (count) · loopability_score (viral spread potential). PERFORMANCE = attention coherence and durability.
TEASE_CONVERSION_ENGINE
S2+S3A · Value delay economics
Formal model of information delay economics. Two delay types: STRUCTURAL (information genuinely unavailable — legitimate patience required) vs MANUFACTURED (information withheld to maintain engagement and extract capital commitment). tease_intensity [0-10], delay_structure, conversion_pressure [PASSIVE/NUDGED/AGGRESSIVE], reward_control [OPEN/CONTROLLED/WITHHELD]. Rule: tease > delivery → raise FAKE_INERTIA probability, reduce trust. "Manufactured delay" is the economic engine behind many narrative bubbles.
Distinct from: adversarial_intent_classifier (manipulation intent) · IMPRESSION_MANAGEMENT (emitter archetype). TEASE_CONVERSION = the economic MECHANISM of how delay is structured.
RULE_REGIME_CLASSIFIER
S0+S3A · Governing rule set
Classifies which rule set dominates the current signal environment. GLOBAL (international norms, standard financial rules — all participants on same framework). HYBRID (global framework with significant local carve-outs). LOCAL (country-specific, platform-specific, or cultural rules dominate — participants using global models will systematically misread). High misalignment when LOCAL contradicts GLOBAL assumptions. Rule: HIGH misalignment → adapt interpretation or exit.
Distinct from: regime_portability_score (does signal survive regime shift?) · war_style_classifier (what type of game?). RULE_REGIME = what are the governing rules RIGHT NOW.
AGENCY_LEVEL_GATE
S35+S7 · Participant autonomy · Most critical
Formal gate on market participant autonomy. INTACT = voluntary decisions, genuine price discovery. WEAKENING = structural pressures reducing voluntary participation (moderate forced flows, mild margin calls, index rebalancing). LOST = structural compulsion dominates — forced sellers, redemption cascades, regulatory liquidations. When LOST: no genuine narrative signal present. Only structural flows. Narrative analysis produces zero edge. New decision engine condition: Agency ≠ LOST.
Distinct from: liquidity_buffer_gate (capital availability) · self_reinforcing_cascade_flag (cascade). AGENCY_LEVEL = participant AUTONOMY — are people choosing to act or compelled to act?
Agency level gate — why it's the most operationally critical
When agency is LOST: all other lenses produce noise, not signal
INTACT
Participants making voluntary decisions. Price discovery functioning. Narrative signals are meaningful. Full pipeline evaluation applies.
WEAKENING
Moderate structural pressure. Some forced flows present but voluntary participants still dominant. Reduce conviction by one level. SNIPER only.
LOST → NO ENTRY
Forced sellers/buyers dominate. Margin calls, redemption cascades, regulatory compulsion. Narrative analysis is irrelevant — only structural flows matter. Do not enter regardless of signal quality.
Detection signals: bid-ask spreads spiking without news (forced selling), large volume with no narrative catalyst (index rebalancing), sharp directional move with no Kalshi/Polymarket odds change (structural, not narrative-driven).

V5.6 is the most productive lens iteration since the V5.2 patch — 4 genuine additions from the 5 new lenses, with 1 already covered. The 10 lenses from V5.5 all remain exactly mapped. The new additions fill four specific gaps.

AGENCY_LEVEL_GATE is the most operationally critical. When market participants have lost autonomy — forced sellers, redemption cascades, margin call liquidations dominate — narrative analysis produces zero edge. The signal the pipeline detects is real but it doesn't matter because the price moves are structural, not narrative-driven. A narrative-intelligence engine that fires in AGENCY_LOST conditions will consistently produce false signals. The gate fires as a hard blocker in the decision engine: Agency ≠ LOST is now the 7th of 9 conditions required for ACT. Detection signals: bid-ask spreads spiking without news, large volume with no Kalshi/Polymarket odds change, sharp directional moves with no accompanying narrative catalyst.

TEASE_CONVERSION_ENGINE formalises the economic model of manufactured delay. The key distinction is STRUCTURAL delay (information genuinely not yet available) vs MANUFACTURED delay (information available but withheld to maintain engagement). The tease > delivery rule directly connects to FAKE_INERTIA probability — when the narrative promises more than it delivers but keeps the crowd engaged, that is the economic engine of narrative bubbles. Most speculation cycles have a manufactured delay component at their peak.

PERFORMANCE_LENS adds attention quality. Signal_density measures how many signals exist. Pent_up_attention measures suppressed attention volume. PERFORMANCE measures whether crowd attention to THIS narrative is coherent and durable — focused, sticky, and precisely timed — or fragmented, fading, and off. A SILENT_BUILD signal with FOCUSED attention quality is a stronger setup than the same signal with FRAGMENTED attention. RULE_REGIME_CLASSIFIER adds the governing rule set dimension — LOCAL rule dominance with GLOBAL-framework participants creates systematic misread conditions.
