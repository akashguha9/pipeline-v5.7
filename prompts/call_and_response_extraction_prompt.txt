── SIGNAL CALLS ──────────────────────────────────────────────

── REGIME CALLS ──────────────────────────────────────────────

CALL-R1: What does the content suggest about the current macro
regime — risk-on, risk-off, or transitional? What specific

── ACTOR CALLS ───────────────────────────────────────────────

CALL-A1: Which specific actors (governments, central banks,
companies, individuals) made commitments or statements in the
files? For each: what did they commit to, and is there any
evidence they will or won't deliver?

CALL-A2: Are there any cases in the files where an actor's
stated position differs from their revealed behaviour?

— A result mistaken for a repeatable process?
— A narrative mistaken for a mechanism?
— Rival weakness mistaken for self-strength?
— Slow bleed that was missed until too late?

CALL-B3: What does the content suggest about how operators
(traders, investors, decision-makers) are currently behaving?
Are they overconfident, under-positioned, or in denial?
