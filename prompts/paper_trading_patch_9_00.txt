── 1A. AROUSAL STATE — NEW SESSION-OPENING GATE ─────────────

Before anything else each session, classify:

  DORMANT:   No signal intake. Archive review only. No ENTER.
  BUILDING:  Signal intake + ghost layer only. No ENTER.
  ACTIVE:    Full pipeline. All size tiers available.
  PEAK:      LEICESTER or UNION_TYPE only. QUARTER_UNIT max.
  DECLINING: Existing positions review only. No new ENTER.
  COLLAPSE:  EXIT protocol for flagged positions.

Classification inputs (all three required):
  Market: emotional_charge_quadrant arousal + crowd_sync
          + narrative_phase energy
  Signal: loopability velocity + shock acceleration direction
  Operator: your own state
    HIGH_NOREPI  -> forces PEAK regardless of market
    DEPLETED     -> forces DORMANT regardless of market
    HIGH_DOPAMINE -> elevate market reading by one level

Log at start of every session: arousal_state = [state]

─── 1B. EMOTIONAL CHARGE QUADRANT — WAR_STYLE PRE-ROUTING ───

Score every signal on 2 axes before TransmissionTestNode:
  AROUSAL [0-10]: how urgent/shocking is this?
  VALENCE [0-10]: fear/threat (low) vs opportunity/greed (high)

Four quadrants with direct war_style routing:
  CRISIS    (high arousal + low valence):  KAMIKAZE
             Fast decay. phi~0.40. Small size. Urgent exit.
  EUPHORIA  (high arousal + high valence): Check BINGE_PATTERN
             first. If genuine ATLETICO: ATTRITION. If not: LEICESTER.
  DREAD     (low arousal + low valence):  TASTE_MIGRATION
             phi~0.95. Long horizon. Patience required.
  COMPLACENCY (low arousal + high valence): LEAVE immediately.
             No narrative energy = no edge. Do not assess further.

Live examples:
  Iran invasion 64% = CRISIS quadrant -> KAMIKAZE routing for
    new entries in that theme. (Explains why LEICESTER_TYPE
    sizing on new positions, not ATLETICO.)
  CVX oil divergence = DREAD quadrant -> TASTE_MIGRATION
    routing -> long time_horizon justified.

── 1C. IRG FORMULA — ILLUSION-REALITY GAP DIAGNOSTIC ────────

Before sizing any signal, compute IRG:

  IRG = (credibility_construction + narrative_heat)
        - (constraint_reality + flow_confirmation)

Where:
  credibility_construction:
    FOLK=2 / ENDORSED=5 / STANDARDIZED=8 / TAKEN_FOR_GRANTED=10
    (from construct_legitimation_tracker)

  narrative_heat:
    loopability_score x crowd_synchronization_score / 10

  constraint_reality:
    10 - PoNR_implementation
    (low implementation = high constraint = high constraint_reality)

  flow_confirmation:
    synchronized_attention_flag x type_weight
    HUB_TRIGGERED=0.4 / COMPLEX_CONTAGION=0.8

Routing:
  IRG > 6:  ILLUSION LIKELY. Structural read overrides narrative.
            PoNR_announcement discounted 40%.
            NARRATIVE conviction component penalised.
            fragility_index automatically elevated.

  IRG 3-6:  MIXED. Apply dual_read_protocol. Both reads partial.

  IRG < 3:  GROUNDED. Narrative and structure aligned.
            Standard entry conditions.

Live example: Iran invasion
  credibility=5 (ENDORSED) + narrative_heat~7 = 12
  constraint_reality=7 (PoNR_impl~3) + flow_conf=0.4 (HUB) = 7.4
  IRG = 12 - 7.4 = 4.6 -> MIXED
  This is why oil hasn't moved. Market already pricing IRG correctly.

── 1D. CONVICTION SCORE DECOMPOSITION ───────────────────────

When leave_score >= 7, decompose WHERE conviction comes from:

  LOGICAL:       transmission_test quality x PoNR_impl x VRIN
  NARRATIVE:     loopability x construct_legitimation x narrative_phase
  EMOTIONAL:     emotional_charge_quadrant x crowd_sync
  INSTITUTIONAL: adhesive_capital x archetype_anchors confirmed

fragility_index [0-10]:
  HIGH when: NARRATIVE conviction >> LOGICAL conviction
             (story running ahead of mechanism)
  HIGH when: EMOTIONAL conviction >> INSTITUTIONAL conviction
             (crowd ahead of structure)
  LOW when: all four sources roughly balanced

Hard sizing gates from fragility_index:
  fragility_index > 6: HALF_UNIT maximum regardless of leave_score
  fragility_index > 8: QUARTER_UNIT maximum + anti_delusion MANDATORY

Live example: GS/SpaceX thesis
  NARRATIVE conviction HIGH (89% Polymarket odds)
  LOGICAL conviction LOW (binary event, no structural mechanism)
  INSTITUTIONAL conviction LOW (low adhesive_capital vs Musk)
  fragility_index = HIGH -> HALF_UNIT cap confirmed. Correct sizing.

── 1E. PERFORMER-OPERATOR MISALIGNMENT SCORE ────────────────

Before any thesis assessment, identify the 3-way split:
  PERFORMER: who is telling the story
  AUDIENCE:  who is meant to believe and act
  OPERATOR:  who actually benefits structurally

Score misalignment [0-10] using:
  credential_gap:      performer have genuine structural knowledge?
  incentive_direction: performer benefit BEFORE or AFTER reveal?
  audience_asymmetry:  audience less informed than operator?
  operator_visibility: real beneficiary visible or hidden?

Routing:
  0-3 (LOW):   Standard pipeline. Narrative read reliable.
  4-6 (MEDIUM): NARRATIVE conviction reduced 30%.
  7-10 (HIGH):  NARRATIVE conviction unreliable.
                PoNR_announcement discounted 40%.
                IMPLEMENTATION_GAP_OVERWEIGHT risk elevated.

Live example: Iran invasion narrative
  Performers = media/political actors
  Operators = regional power actors + defense contractors
  Misalignment = MEDIUM (performers have incentive to amplify)
  -> PoNR_announcement discount applied. Explains CVX oil
     not moving despite 64% invasion probability.

PART 2 — ROUTING CHANGES (how signals are classified)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

── 2A. INFORMATION ABSORPTION SPEED — TAG EVERY SIGNAL ───────

Tag each signal with how fast the market can price it:

  FAST:       Widely legible. Many participants can act immediately.
              Examples: rate decisions, earnings surprises, election
              results, viral social media.
              Rule: require PoNR_implementation >= 4 before entry.
              Cannot front-run — market processes these in hours.

  MEDIUM:     Requires sector knowledge or relationship access.
              Examples: Form 4 patterns, supply chain shifts,
              prediction market probability changes.
              Standard pipeline applies.

  SLOW:       Requires deep domain expertise or synthesis across
              domains. Weeks to months.
              Examples: geopolitical thesis building, narrative
              formation pre-consensus, phi decay estimation.
              Highest edge window. DISCOVERY tier eligible.

  STRUCTURAL: Never fully absorbed. Embedded in market architecture.
              Examples: ghost pools, survivorship bias, denominator
              gaps.
              Permanently mispriced. ghost_layer_protocol mandatory.

Apply at admission. FAST signals with PoNR_impl < 4 = WATCH only.

── 2B. NARRATIVE PHASE — ENERGY WITHIN A STAGE ──────────────

Every thesis now has TWO dimensions:
  Stage: PRE-NARRATIVE / FORMATION / EXPLOSION / SATURATION
  Phase: IGNITION / BUILD / EXPANSION / PEAK / DECAY / RESET

The combination is more informative than either alone.

Critical combination to watch:
  EXPLOSION stage + DECAY phase = MOST DANGEROUS POSITION
  Thesis still looks active (many participants, broad coverage)
  but energy is leaving. This is where most late entries happen.
  -> Exit discipline elevated even if narrative still loud.

  FORMATION stage + IGNITION phase = HIGHEST ASYMMETRY
  Fresh energy in early-stage thesis.
  -> Strongest edge potential. SLOW absorption likely.

  Any stage + PEAK phase = CROWDED warning
  -> post_game_lock check. Reduce sizing.

  Any stage + RESET phase = DISCOVERY scan trigger
  -> Watch for new signals in that class.

── 2C. CROWD SYNCHRONIZATION QUALITY ────────────────────────

synchronized_attention_flag (binary >= 3 of 4) is still the
admission gate. Add this quality check when it fires:

  crowd_synchronization_score [0-10]:
    Inputs: platform convergence + participant heterogeneity
            + temporal alignment + signal coherence

  Critical routing distinction:
    HIGH score + LOW heterogeneity:
      Echo chamber. Fragile synchrony despite high score.
      -> KAMIKAZE routing (HUB_TRIGGERED subtype)

    HIGH score + HIGH heterogeneity:
      Genuine synchrony across different participant types.
      -> ATTRITION routing (COMPLEX_CONTAGION subtype)

  Detection: if 3 of 4 platforms showing the signal are all
  the same type (e.g. 3 crypto Twitter accounts) = LOW
  heterogeneity even with flag fired.

── 2D. PERCEIVED USEFULNESS GATE ────────────────────────────

When a thesis PASSES transmission test but has any of:
  - Specialist knowledge required to understand mechanism
  - Collective action required before mechanism fires
  - Infrastructure not yet in place

Apply: extend time_horizon +1 tier
  SHORT -> MEDIUM
  MEDIUM -> LONG

Do NOT leave the thesis. Mechanism is correct. Repricing
is delayed by adoption friction, not by thesis being wrong.

Live example: BTC digital-gold secondary thesis
  Mechanism valid. Institutional adoption = specialist + collective
  action required (new mental models, new products).
  -> MEDIUM->LONG time_horizon extension.
  -> Supports MIGRATING classification with patience.

── 2E. FEEDBACK DELAY CLASSIFIER ────────────────────────────

Every thesis has a loop structure. Classify at entry:

  SHORT_LOOP: hours/days. Overshoot/reversal risk.
    -> KAMIKAZE routing. Strict exit at CROWDED.
    Examples: meme stocks, crypto rumor pumps.

  MEDIUM_LOOP: weeks/months. Standard.
    -> ATTRITION or HUMPED_CURVE.
    Examples: policy cycles, earnings, RTX defense thesis.

  LONG_LOOP: quarters/years. Structural.
    -> TASTE_MIGRATION (phi~0.95). Patience required.
    PoNR_implementation will be very slow.
    Examples: VNM Vietnam FDI thesis.

  BROKEN_LOOP: disrupted. May never close.
    -> STRUCTURAL absorption. Mark WATCH permanently.
    Thesis may be correct but unpriceable.

Distinct from war_style (narrative decay) — feedback_delay
classifies the CAUSAL LOOP of the underlying mechanism.

── 2F. REVEAL COMPRESSION WINDOW ────────────────────────────

For every significant thesis, log:
  trigger:             specific observable event that forces
                       narrative-reality reconciliation
  timeframe:           SHORT/MEDIUM/LONG based on feedback_delay
  repricing_intensity: HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW

repricing_intensity = HIGH when:
  IRG was HIGH before reveal (large gap = violent snap)
  AND performer_operator_misalignment HIGH (complex unwinding)
  AND narrative_phase was PEAK at reveal (max crowd exposure)

Exit discipline:
  HIGH intensity reveal -> exit BEFORE reveal, not after
  (crowd already fully positioned by the time reveal hits)
  Adjust stop placement based on expected intensity.

  time_horizon must INCLUDE the reveal, not end before it.

Live example: RTX
  trigger = confirmed defence budget increase or military action
  timeframe = MEDIUM
  repricing_intensity = HIGH (IRG=MIXED, narrative BUILD phase,
    not yet PEAK, crowd not fully in yet)
  -> Stay in through the reveal event itself.

── 3A. EDGAR / FILING ATTENTION SIGNAL ──────────────────────

When EDGAR data is available, check for behavioral signals
BEFORE reading filing content (these are upstream of content lag):

  (a) Abnormal IP count on 10-K within 48hrs of filing:
      Informed agents are paying to process this.
      -> Upgrade transmission confidence +1 tier.
      -> Extend time_horizon: SLOW absorption signal.

  (b) First-minute web visits to Form 4 filings:
      Insider activity attracting rapid attention.
      -> Information_absorption_speed = MEDIUM-FAST.
      -> Check insider direction for thesis confirmation.

  (c) Simultaneous current + historical filing access:
      Deep due diligence in progress (not media reaction).
      -> Institutional actor processing comprehensively.
      -> SLOW absorption with durable edge window.

High filing_attention + complex filing + small/opaque firm
= strongest EDGAR behavioral signal combination.

── 3B. NOISE AUDIT — EVERY 20 CLOSED TRADES ─────────────────

When T_closed reaches 20, 40, 60... run noise audit:

  Level noise check:
    Do certain signal types (crypto, geopolitical, defense)
    systematically receive different leave_scores regardless
    of actual quality?
    If yes -> recalibrate baseline per signal type.

  Occasion noise check:
    Does the same signal type score differently depending on
    when in the session it's assessed?
    If yes -> impose fixed assessment order across sessions.

  Pattern noise check:
    Does HIGH_DOPAMINE operator state produce systematically
    higher leave_scores vs BALANCED for equivalent signals?
    If yes -> apply operator state correction factor.

Output: NONE / LEVEL / OCCASION / PATTERN
Log in Moltbook. Apply remediation before next session.

── 3C. PQS CALIBRATION AUDIT — EVERY 10 SESSIONS ───────────

When session count reaches 10, 20, 30... run:

  (1) Blind re-score: re-assess 3 prior positions without
      looking at original scores. If they diverge > 1 point
      consistently -> OCCASION_NOISE in PQS scoring.

  (2) Cross-pattern check: do certain asset types always
      score higher on setup_quality or protocol_compliance
      regardless of actual quality?
      If yes -> PATTERN_NOISE in PQS scoring.

  (3) Revision cadence check: what percentage of positions
      had at least one PQS revision between entry and close?
      Low rate = Tetlock violation. Update: reassess more.

Why this matters: if PQS scores are noisy, MW is updating
its 22+ weights on noisy signal data. Garbage in, garbage out.
Clean PQS -> cleaner MW -> better calibration by May 31.
