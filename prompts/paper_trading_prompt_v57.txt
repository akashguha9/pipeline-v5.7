SECTION 1 — PRE-ENTRY PHASE CLASSIFICATION (NEW — run first)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Before anything else, classify the signal's PHASE:

PRE_NARRATIVE_PHASE (temporal_arbitrage_score ×3):
  Prediction market drifting >5% over 4+ hours
  WITHOUT a major news catalyst
  = Informed participants moving before public narrative
  = Highest-value entry window

NARRATIVE_PHASE (temporal_arbitrage_score ×1.5):
  Public narrative forming, signal visible, crowd engaging
  Standard FIRE_MODE evaluation applies

TERMINAL_PHASE (temporal_arbitrage_score ×0.3):
  Event has occurred and is confirmed
  Market pricing reflects it
  = Most value already extracted
  = Reduce leave_score by 4, rarely worth entry

RULE: Log the phase at every entry. ROI by phase is the most
important calibration signal for May → June 1 paste.

SECTION 7 — CLV CALIBRATION METHODOLOGY (NEW)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT IS CLV HERE:
  CLV = entry_price vs final market VWAP at resolution close
  Positive CLV = you were better than the final market price = PROCESS_WIN
  Negative CLV = market priced it better than you = PROCESS_LOSS

HOW MW UPDATE USES CLV (70/30 weighting):
  PROCESS_WIN + outcome WIN: full upward MW update
  PROCESS_WIN + outcome LOSS: 70% upward update (process right, variance)
  PROCESS_LOSS + outcome WIN: 30% upward update (luck, not process)
  PROCESS_LOSS + outcome LOSS: full downward MW update

BENCHMARK: Polymarket/Kalshi VWAP in final 4 hours before resolution
  = equivalent of Pinnacle closing line in sports betting

TARGET FOR MAY: Build 30-trade CLV baseline before June 1 paste.
  This is more important than the raw P&L outcome series.
