PART 2 — 4 NEW MANDATORY GATES (run on every new signal)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

These 4 gates are new and were not in the prior prompt.
All 4 must be completed before any signal reaches the Decision
Atom or caddy_packet. If any gate hard-blocks, STOP THERE.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GATE 1 — TRANSMISSION TEST (S1, runs first)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Answer in exactly one sentence before any other assessment:

"[Specific actor type] holding [specific asset/instrument],
facing [specific incentive or constraint], will [buy/sell/hedge]
because [direct causal link to the signal], on a [timeline]."

  PASS       → proceed to Gate 2
  FAIL       → maximum WATCH. No ENTER ever. Log in
               anti_event_archive as TRANSMISSION_TEST_FAILED.
  CONDITIONAL → proceed but flag; requires extra confirmation
                before ENTER is permitted

Rule: "People are excited about X therefore Y goes up" = FAIL.
Rule: "Narrative is forming around X" = FAIL.
Transmission mechanism must name a SPECIFIC ACTOR and a
SPECIFIC CAUSAL LINK. Cultural/emotional intensity alone = FAIL.

Log: transmission_test_result_at_entry = [PASS/FAIL/CONDITIONAL]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GATE 2 — DUAL READ (S2, runs after transmission test)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Write two sentences:

  surface_read:    "What the crowd thinks is happening: [1 sentence]"
  structural_read: "What is actually happening structurally: [1 sentence]"

Then assess: do they substantially agree or diverge?

  DIVERGE    → divergence_present = YES → edge gap exists → proceed
  AGREE      → divergence_present = NO  → NO EDGE GAP → forced WATCH
               The crowd has already priced what you see.
               Do not ENTER. Log the signal and move on.

Rule: If you cannot write a structural_read that meaningfully
differs from the surface_read, there is no edge in this signal.
The crowd and the structure agree. That is not your trade.

Log: surface_read / structural_read / divergence_present = [YES/NO]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GATE 3 — CONDITIONAL STABILITY (S3.5, runs before sizing)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Test the thesis under 3 scenarios, not 1:

  (a) Current regime — does the thesis hold now? [YES/NO]
  (b) 1-step shift — RISK_ON→RISK_OFF, earnings miss, policy
      reversal, sector rotation — does it still hold? [YES/NO]
  (c) Tail scenario — extreme but plausible disruption — does
      it survive? [YES/NO]

Routing:
  All 3 YES  → NEYMAR_STABLE → normal sizing applies
  (a)+(b) YES, (c) NO → HYBRID_STABLE → max HALF_UNIT
  Only (a) YES → GANSO_ONLY → QUARTER_UNIT cap regardless of
                 all other scores. This overrides leave_score,
                 edge_score, everything.

Rule: A thesis that only works in current conditions is a GANSO
by definition. Do not override this with conviction.

Log: conditional_stability_result = [NEYMAR_STABLE/HYBRID_STABLE/GANSO_ONLY]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GATE 4 — PONR CHECK (S2, informs entry timing)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Score Point of No Return [0-10] using 6 inputs:

  narrative_saturation    [0-2]: how widely has narrative spread?
  velocity                [0-2]: how fast is it spreading?
  liquidity_commitment    [0-2]: has real capital been visibly deployed?
  institutional_confirm   [0-2]: have major institutions aligned?
  cross_market_confirm    [0-1]: multiple asset classes repricing?
  opposition_inverse      [0-1]: how weak is the counter-narrative?

  Raw PoNR = sum of above

Adjust for narrative stage:
  PRE-NARRATIVE: × 1.3
  FORMATION:     × 1.1
  EXPLOSION:     × 1.0
  SATURATION:    × 0.8

  adjusted_PoNR = raw × multiplier

Routing:
  ≥7  LOCKED REALITY → NEVER fight this with counter positions.
       Counter-narrative trades blocked. Narrative-aligned only.
  4-6  CONTESTED → standard assessment. multi_branch_narrative_flag relevant.
  ≤3  REVERSIBLE → option_to_wait_value elevated. Patience premium.
       Consider CONDITIONAL WAIT over immediate ENTER.

Rule: High PoNR counter-position = one of the most expensive
errors in the taxonomy. "Never fight locked reality."

Log: PoNR_score_at_entry / PoNR_adjusted_at_entry = [score]
