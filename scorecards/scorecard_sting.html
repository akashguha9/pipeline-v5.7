BEHAVIORAL_INTEGRITY_SCORER (BIS)
Longitudinal tracking of operator conduct consistency across sessions. The pipeline knows what good conduct IS (COMPAS beats, CVS checks, Moltbook logging, ENTRY_DESIRE_LOG). It doesn't track whether the operator executes these consistently over time. BIS tracks per-session: ENTRY_DESIRE_LOG completed, Beat 0 run, CVS computed, no CHAOS_ENTRY, SELF_MODEL consulted, Moltbook logged, BCS gap ≤ 0.3. BIS_session = items_completed/total. BIS_rolling = EMA(α=0.3). If BIS_rolling < 0.6: CONDUCT_DEGRADATION flag → PROBE-only mode. If BIS_rolling > 0.85 for 10+ consecutive sessions: CONDUCT_ANCHOR → earned GHOST_STANCE_EMERGENCE. BIS_trend < 0 (declining): early warning signal — April 4 CHAOS_ENTRY cluster would have been preceded by declining BIS (shortcuts, missing Beat 0, incomplete Moltbook). 'Manners × cross-context consistency = true character.' BIS IS that cross-context consistency metric.

OUTSIDER_ADVANTAGE_DETECTOR (OAD)
Formal scoring of when being counter-consensus creates HIGHER expected value than conforming. ADVERSARIAL_OCCUPANCY asks 'is the adversary in my position?' OAD asks 'when does the absence of the crowd FROM my position create edge?' OAD = C_internal × (1 − D_consensus) − P_conformity × 0.3. D_consensus = how aligned is the current narrative with mainstream. C_internal = thesis quality independent of market consensus (COMPOSITE_EDGE × (1−D_t) × L(t)). When OAD > 0.3: OUTSIDER_ADVANTAGE — internal coherence AND market ignoring it = full edge. The Englishman in New York state: misread by the dominant culture, internally coherent, standing at a genuine mispricing. RTX had moderate OAD. A hypothetical Polymarket market where 90% says NO but your high-C_internal thesis says YES = maximum OAD window. OAD adds: even leave_score of 7.5 may justify SNIPER when OAD is high — the edge is in the market's misreading, not just signal strength.
